Previews

This page offers fully interactive previews of my QR code generators. They are entirely free and open source and do all the work in your browser. This means the text you type will not be sent to a remote server. It also enables you to download them and use them on a computer not connected to the internet. You can click the link above a preview to jump to the corresponding website.
